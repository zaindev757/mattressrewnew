Frequently Asked Questions

Find your questions answered here.

How does Mattress Renew work?

Mattress Renew has 12 separate air chambers that can be custom inflated to fix the sag in the specific areas of your mattress needing help. See instructions PDF on this page for more information.

What size mattresses does it work for?

Mattress Renew works for every size mattress, from twin to king. Simply inflate the air chambers that are needed for your mattress and leave the unneeded sections uninflated. You will need to fold some of the uninflated sections underneath for full & twin. Depending on the sagging areas of king size mattresses, two Mattress Renew products may be required. Only one will be required if the mattress is sagging in the center of your mattress.

Will it work for my mattress?

The only beds that Mattress Renew will not work for are Sleep Number beds. Otherwise, Mattress Renew will work well for all innerspring and memory foam mattresses.

Will Mattress Renew deflate and require more air?

Probably not. Mattress Renew has been specifically designed to hold air virtually indefinitely. We manufacture Mattress Renew with high quality .32 mm PVC vinyl and it has been rigorously tested under extreme conditions. The narrow inflation channels are also designed to self seal, and our valves require a squeeze of the thumb and forefinger to release air.

What are the dimensions?

The overall dimensions of Mattress Renew is 55” wide x 60” long when fully opened. The unused outer chambers can be folded underneath for queen-size, full size and twin size mattresses.

How does the 30 day money back guarantee work?

You must contact us within 30 days of the original order date to receive a return authorization approval. Original shipping & handling charges will not be refunded and it is up to the customer to pay for return shipping.
